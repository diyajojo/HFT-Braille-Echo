This website focuses on two major areas. It translates braille to english as well as scans braille texts and coverts it to audio. It helps visually impaired individuals scan Braille text with their smartphone camera, converting it into spoken words or text on the screen. It enhances accessibility by enabling independent comprehension of printed materials like menus and books

bridging the gap between the visually impaired and the printed world.
